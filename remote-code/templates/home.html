Motivation

Market analysis for the work market has been a highly researched domain for many years now. People have tried to find answers to questions such as: 'What is the link between what you know and how much you earn?', 'Does the country you live in influence employers to choose you?', 'Is it the way you express yourself that makes you a good choice for a project?', 'What skills can you learn to be more successful?'. Our project aims to answer exactly these types of questions, regarding the market of online freelancing. This webpage displays our results.

Dataset

We have picked Freelancer.com to be the platform that provides the dataset. It is a web platform that allows employers to post projects and find people that can work on them and helps freelancers find projects to work on. The reason why we chose to work with data from a freelancing website is because analyzing the projects and the skills required on the freelancing market also provides a better understanding of where the entire market is heading.

We decided to work with projects from only three categories so that we have a more connected network and thus, a more speciffic analysis. The three chosen categories belong to the field of Computer Science and are: Websites, IT & Software, Mobile Phones & Computing and Design, Media & Architecture. The data was gathered during 5 days. We have downloaded 1.770.031 projects, 29.965 project details, 28.640 sellers, 363.884 bids and 45.399 users.
